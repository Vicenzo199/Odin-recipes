AGATOGO


Description

Agatoga is a vegan stew made mainly from plantains. This stew is loaded with flavor and uses vegetables like carrots, bell peppers, and tomatoes along with the plantains. Agatoga is an African stew that is not just tasteful but is also super easy to make and healthy.

its rich and warm flavor offers comfort and is perfect for cold seasons. Make this stew in large batches and serve it at gatherings and parties.

Ingredients


  • Avocado oil
  • Green plantains
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • garlic

Steps


  1. First of all, you will need to heat avocado oil in a saucepan or a pot
  2. Once the oil heats up, Add chopped onion and sauté for 2 to 3 minutes or until they are tender and translucent
  3. Now, add garlic, tomatoes, and red bell pepper. Cook for 3 more minutes or until the tomatoes are soft. Add tomato paste and mix well
  4. Next, add sliced green plantains, carrots, vegan bouillon cubes, Bragg’s liquid aminos, onion powder, garlic powder and mix well

UGALI RECIPE



Description


Ugali is a dish made by cooking maize flour (course cornmeal similar to polenta in texture) into a type of mush. It is a popular meal in most African countries where it goes by different names.


Ingredients


  • coarse cornmeal
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add cornmeal, a little at a time and stir.
  3. Continue stirring, the mixture will form a dough-like consistency as it cooks. Break any lumps that form by pressing them on the sides of your pot.
